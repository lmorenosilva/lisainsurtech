WHAT IS LISA INSURTECH?

We are the strategic ally that provides the insurance industry with innovative AI technology in order to enhance and improve all its processes from start to finish.

LISA Insurtech accelerates traditional insurance processes which guarantees efficiency, speed, cost savings and security from start to finish.

We seek to create a change that redirects the insurance industry to a new paradigm far from its traditionality and assures all people with well-being and tranquility to face an uncertain future.
